Mahalli Por is a platform I'm currently developing to help companies in Saudi Arabia measure and improve their local content score.

It collects a company's operational and procurement data, calculates the local content percentage against the relevant criteria, and then generates clear, practical recommendations on where and how the company can raise its score.

The goal is to turn local content — often treated as a complex compliance exercise — into something measurable and actionable, giving businesses a concrete roadmap for improvement.
